{{< poem >}}
I see the joyful wrinkles in the leather,
Reclining in chaotic peace with you,
What outpost in such daring seas adrift,
What fortress, buttress of the truth we find,
This band of brothers clothed in laughs so freely given.
{{< /poem >}}
