{{< poem >}}
The seed of longing underfoot,
So born from melodies - the drought,
Of which blooms heady plumes of soot,
The tail on which a fire tauts,
It's beauty keeps so patiently restrained,
Ascends a smoke of longing heavenward.
{{< /poem >}}
