{{< poem >}}
In all these misty bogs, the deepest mire,
I am so unburdened at the silly sight:
Colluding tweens and teens do sum again,
Eleven sum fourteen usurp twenty five thrones,
How ever will they choose a seat?!
Surely time will tell.
{{< /poem >}}
