{{< poem >}}
Wild plains in overlooking looms,
With peacefully unkempt desire-
The myriads of pearls in breaking tide,
The whimsy drawing thick upon the edge,
Of all the world in painted evening hues,
Of all the world so desperately in love with You.
{{< /poem >}}
