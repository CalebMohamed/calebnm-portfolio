{{< poem >}}
For just a moment in the breeze,
Such ordinary beauty'd melt the heart,
So deaf and blind I've been to these,
I longed but so forgot:
A birdsong and it's praise,
The Grace that bows the winds as instruments.
{{< /poem >}}
