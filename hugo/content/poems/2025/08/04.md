{{< poem >}}
How nice to share a wrap with you,
In this chaotic life.
It needn't be so easy though,
Need not be so sorted.
Need not be so thinly pleasant,
Our lives run deeper still,
To hidden sites eternal and unbreachable.
{{< /poem >}}
