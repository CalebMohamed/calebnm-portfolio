{{< poem >}}
The shallows of the shade,
Swim circles underfoot,
By root and jumbled sage,
Play mimes if cared to look,

Oh imitate the day,
By wake of bubbling brook,
And image as we pray,
A life in faithful foot.
{{< /poem >}}
