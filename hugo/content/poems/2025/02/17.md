{{< poem >}}
The ever-whirr arises from the grave of sound,
Unfathoming from every plug,
Dripping off the hooks of sprawling wire,
Pervading like the hollow of a bad dream,
In unwilled passing fades to a subtle hiss.
{{< /poem >}}
