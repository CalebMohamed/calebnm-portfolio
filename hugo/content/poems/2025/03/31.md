{{< poem >}}
Surprised by weighted face,
A slumber comes as if to one:
Slumbering whilst awake. But still,
The flicker stays behind my eyes,
Though muffled through an endless fog,
I've seemed to draw an overdraft too dire.
{{< /poem >}}
