{{< poem >}}
Oak banisters that turn two ways in passing,
Ascending at the others fall,
Within a ceaseless maze departing,
Truly zero sum enthralled,
Pore over cryptic clues remarking,
Ever span these winding walls.
{{< /poem >}}
