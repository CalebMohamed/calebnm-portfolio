{{< poem >}}
Full of stories from a far off plain,
A fiercer Sun on windswept flat,
Talk of bricks back when your crown was black,
But now you have a silver mane,
Now a twinkle that still sits behind,
Your wrinkled eyes.
{{< /poem >}}
