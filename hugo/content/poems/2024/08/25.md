{{< poem >}}
I am tired of your pictures,
Funny figures circle-dancing,
Why discard your caricatures,
At my feet until you're laughing,
So I ask you for a moment,
Would you care to hear my ranting?
No-gui~ No-gui~!
No-gui~ No-gui~!
