{{< poem >}}
The foil of haste has wrecked,
Upon my frame that I inspect,
Ill-fallen limbs and swollen neck,
Now haste is dead. Kicked up but specks,
Of dust and fury sharpening the edge of coughs.
{{< /poem >}}
