{{< poem >}}
Where is the poem?
Not now I assumed,
But as now leapt on,
I ponder anew,
Is the poem now here,
Unfinished but due,
Awaiting full stop,
Each poem's sole muse,
For every poem must end,
To this end its true,
Where is the poem?
The poem's now with you.
