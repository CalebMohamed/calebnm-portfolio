{{< poem >}}
Library grind,
Head down, hands busy,
Striking keys, writing neatly,
I'm looking to find,
Tasks done just maybe,
Ahead of the game, I've been lately,
Behind.

I see the cusp,
The mountain peaking beneath the gorgeous sky,
That makes small the wandering heights.
