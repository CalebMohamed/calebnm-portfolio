# 22.04.23
Heckled head,  
And thoughts seem to flow like a glacier,  
A lukewarm brain-freeze stills my tired temples,  
And as the memories seem to solidify I leave these planted seeds in your care,  
For you Lord water where you wish.
22.04.23
