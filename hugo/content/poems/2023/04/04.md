# 04.04.23
Cloud walking,  
Sleepy in the air,  
Ascendant on the racing clouds,  
That wrap around the rolling earth,  
And rumble, soaked and heavy in parts,  
And others boil with furious crackles,  
But here they float on slowly now,  
Under the setting stars.
04.04.23
