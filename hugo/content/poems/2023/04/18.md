{{< poem >}}
Busied hands,  
Fall tired on the screen,  
Limp and sleep-primed,  
But first a little more busy tapping,  
And then a handy dream,  
To rest the mind that rhymed,  
And exceed a long time napping.
{{< /poem >}}
