# 09.10.23

Crafting words upon my screen,
What strange diction do I see?
Of public, private, void and true,
Of numbers devoured.
In jaws of brackets, yawning,
Snug against colons on the right,
A script to make the metal dream,

A script to make the metal dream.
09.10.23
