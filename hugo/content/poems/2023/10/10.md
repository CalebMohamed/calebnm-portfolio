{{< poem >}}
If perfection were my master,
I'd write a poem pristine,
And ten out ten I'd long to see,
One zero,
Out of which the crowd erupts,
One zero,
Out of which my Lord outstands,
Two three,
Regardless I hold firm,
By Him I am pristine.
{{< /poem >}}
