# 25.02.23
Wooooooooo!  
A sonic bullet of liquefied enthusiasm soars through the clouds,  
And down, down bellow the parting sea of marshmellows,  
A little boy stands,  
A great joy in his eyes and hope for salvation.
25.02.23
