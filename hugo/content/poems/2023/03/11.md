# 11.03.23
Dancing with my many words,  
Words I borrowed,  
Words I'm told and read aloud,  
Words I'm given to forge and smith,  
So rich a language,  
To smack into shape,  
And to convey so many thoughts,  
With dancing words I will.
11.03.23
