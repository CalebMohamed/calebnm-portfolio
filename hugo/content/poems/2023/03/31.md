{{< poem >}}
Fellowship that never ends,
Ebbing and flowing beyond,
Where darkness is untrue,
Where all is new,

Set right to glorify,
The worthy risen one, 
His in fields abundant,
And there life overflows in fields abundant.
{{< /poem >}}
