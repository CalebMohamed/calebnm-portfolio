# 08.07.23
Frantic hands,
Mind ticking to the clicking beat,
Bomb rushing by,
Gotta think,
Cannot blink,
Find a word to defeat,
The clock ticking as I sit on my seat,
Booooom,
A life lost in the heat -
Of the moment, mind frozen like the peak -
Of mountain and the time is clicking again.
08.07.23
