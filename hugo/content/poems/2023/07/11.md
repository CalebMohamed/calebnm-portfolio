# 11.07.23
Family scramble,
Flying down on a mumbling Dad,
And the duvet is thrown,
A tumble bumble of words,
And a wittering weave is sewn
The window blown -
Right open, see the heat is on high,
And the scramble is now.
*May I have the covers back?*
11.07.23
