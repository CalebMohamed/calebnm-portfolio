# 17.07.23

Digging into down low passes,
Spinning balls off outstretched forearms,
Diving, hitting, falling, missing,
Grasping thoughts to learn the ropes, 
Sometimes grazing speeding fibres,
Nursing cuts and gaping smiles.
17.07.23
