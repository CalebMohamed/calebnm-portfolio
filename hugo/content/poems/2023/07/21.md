Late night trains,
Carriage swaying to the beats of the night,
Quiet humming rising up from the carpet below,
And I sit sunk down to the back of my seat,
Legs tired,
Straight hopping,
Now limp and repeat:
Legs tired,
And we lurch to a stop.
