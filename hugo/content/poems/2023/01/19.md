{{< poem >}}
Hefty bag,  
Laying deep into my shoulders,  
A burden of utility, civility,  
And a little bit of food.  
  
Maybe one day,  
My shoulders in dismay,  
Will sit battered,  
And ponder...  
...the weight of knowledge.
{{< /poem >}}
