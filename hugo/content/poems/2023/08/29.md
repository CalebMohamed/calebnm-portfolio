# 29.08.23

Great Prince of Peace,
Adorned in glory,
Bronzed and steadfast,
Your kingdom claimed in foreign land,

Transcendent Heaven's Lamb,
Adorned in horns and cuts for me,
Tender and worthy,
Your kingdom brought with precious blood.
29.08.23
