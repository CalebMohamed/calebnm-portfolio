{{< poem >}}
Under Your sky,  
And the night returns faithful,  
Silent like a fatherly might,  
Sweeping up the earth in a hug,  
Shoring up like the cosmic father likes to do,  
My day is done,  
My future secure.
{{< /poem >}}
