{{< poem >}}
Dusted done,  
Completed quick,  
And all has said its last for now,  
Dusty falls will sprinkle anew,  
And in time older hands will dust off again,  
And pick up exams and maths galore,  
And dusted done it shall be once more.
{{< /poem >}}
