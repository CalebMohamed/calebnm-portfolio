# 16.06.23
Running round in the cooling eve,  
A fiasco by the swaying trees,  
And out we go,  
One down,  
Two down,  
And alas, I follow,  
Marching to the side to cheer,  
The frenzied shouts I hear,  
Soon we'll be back within the game again.
16.06.23
