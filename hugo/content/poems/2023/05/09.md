# 09.05.23
Socks worn through,  
Maybe it's their last curfew,  
Or rather time to get something new,  
To stop tiny toes going blue,  
And to scatter a few  
Stretchy socks in my draw as a clue,  
A clue for detective feet to cover up and settle down.
09.05.23
