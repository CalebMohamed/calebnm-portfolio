{{< poem >}}
Snuggled up in parents' bed,  
Packaged up in duvet-packing-peanuts,  
That pack us in to talk a little,  
In broken strings of sayings gone,  
I must be going sometime now,  
My bed awaits,  
And poem snuggles end so soon.
{{< /poem >}}
