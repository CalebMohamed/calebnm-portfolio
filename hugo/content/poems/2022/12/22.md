{{< poem >}}
The kitchen lights,  
Shine brighter in dark nights,  
Than warm days and gentle noons,  
They beam and blast,  
Great rays at the dark,  
And seem to make the dark night small,  
And hide in shadows cast.
{{< /poem >}}
