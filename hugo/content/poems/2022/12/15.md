{{< poem >}}
The blues hues,  
Sit lighty in a hazy snooze,  
And all the world is frozen.  
  
The earth and heavens subdued,  
Together in a glacial muse,  
And the flowers are hidden in austere blankets.  
  
The snow is lingering a little longer.
{{< /poem >}}
