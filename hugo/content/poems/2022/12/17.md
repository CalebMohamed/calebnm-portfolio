{{< poem >}}
Around the glowing tree we sit,  
Smiling, laughing, celebrating,  
As the fire's warm and lit,  
We slouch and stand,  
Merry and comfortable,  
Enjoying the company,  
And the time so wonderful.
{{< /poem >}}
