A fragrant summer,  
Breeze drifts lightly,  
To another's slight delight,  
And to my contentment.  
A cozy blanket,  
Falls from the cosmic canopy,  
And raps me up in the deep embrace of stillness.  
It's warm and yellow.  
Another rests beside also.
