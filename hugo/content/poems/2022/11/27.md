{{< poem >}}
Teetering on the edge,  
The fragile balance of sorrow and gladness,  
Is shattered by a stream of immutable joy and peace.  
Tears of love remain.  
Where are you now, shame?  
Are you not cast out by tears of love?
{{< /poem >}}
