# 07.12.23

Where is here?
A place in space,
A step in time,
A still small house by rolling fields,
A wooden frame,
A seated mind,
A world unto itself that introspects,
But I am here,
This... I cannot deny,
I'll crystallise this moment now,
For another moment then.
07.12.23
