# 05.10.23

Trying not to crash and burn,
But touch down smooth,
Just as planes do,

Cease now! I told my tired cries,
But they remained,
Their call the same,

Sooner shall I wrap up lunch,
I tap out rhyme,
I rest the time,

The night delayed will soon run:
As darkness keeps,
Hushed minds asleep.
05.10.23
