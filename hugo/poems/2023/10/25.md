# 25.10.23

Shader dreams,
Like hot wax poured,
Folded at the seams,
As it pools on my screen, 
It hardens as I deem,
Conceived by a script,
Hailing from numbers and machines...

I sit in another world,
The ground is neon,
The sky pulses rhythmic.
25.10.23
