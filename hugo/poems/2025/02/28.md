# 28.02.25

Folding upon folds,
The grey turns frighteningly cold,
Falling from beyond,
Bright's crown to inevitably despond,
Paling light unflinching,
Descends in gradients unattended,
Without flame or flicker as a life,
But still and sterile as a thing.
