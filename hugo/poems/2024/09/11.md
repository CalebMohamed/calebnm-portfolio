# 11.09.24

Lingo flamingo!
Have you come for bingo?
_No, dear one, but bringing lingo,_
Tongues of fire for awed impression,
Skulls for unencumbered laughs,
Modern people in their little windows,
Talk in pictures as they did in ages past.
11.09.24
